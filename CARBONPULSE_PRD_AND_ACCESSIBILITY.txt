PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
CarbonPulse is a smart, personalized carbon assistant designed to help 
individuals estimate their footprint, track daily actions, and get small, 
realistic recommendations to reduce emissions.

Built with: Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, Bun, Prisma ORM
Vertical: Climate Tech & Sustainability Coaching

USER STATEMENT:
Individuals who want to live more sustainably but lack time, expertise, and 
tailored guidance need a simple way to understand, track, and reduce their 
emissions through realistic daily actions so they can form lasting habits and 
see measurable impact without research overload or unrealistic advice.

3. STUDENT COMMUTER (18-25, mobile, income-limited)

4. ECO-MOTIVATED PROFESSIONAL (30-45, wants to lead by example)
   - Strong climate commitment
   - Seeks habit tracking and streak building
   - Wants detailed insights and goal setting
   
5. BUSY PARENT (35-50, time-constrained)
   - Climate concerned but overwhelmed
   - Needs 1-minute check-ins
   - Wants simple family-friendly actions

EXPECTED OUTCOMES:

PROBLEM STATEMENT (EXPANDED)
-----------------------------
Most individuals want to reduce their environmental impact but face three 
root obstacles that prevent action:

1. CALCULATOR ABANDONMENT (Tool Overload)
   - Pain point: Existing carbon calculators demand utility bills, travel 
     receipts, and hours of data entry
   - Result: 70%+ user drop-off before completing first calculation
   - Root cause: High friction conflicts with human impatience
   - User quote: "I want to help but not if I need to dig through my 
     electricity bills"

2. GENERIC RECOMMENDATIONS (Context Mismatch)
   - Pain point: Generic advice ignores real-world constraints 
     (renters can't install solar, students can't afford EVs, low-income 
     families need cost-saving focus)
   - Result: Users feel judged and recommendations feel unrealistic
   - Root cause: One-size-fits-all recommendations don't account for 
     lifestyle, budget, or control constraints
   - User quote: "Stop telling me to buy solar panels—I'm renting"

3. TRACKING FRICTION (Decision Paralysis)
   - Pain point: Users want to log actions but face decision paralysis 
     (what counts? how do I measure? is this impact real?)
   - Result: Tracking abandoned after 2-3 attempts
   - Root cause: No intuitive quick-logging with instant feedback
   - User quote: "I don't know if my effort actually matters"

SOLUTION: CARBONPULSE (Behavior-Change Coach)
----------------------------------------------
CarbonPulse solves these three obstacles by being a supportive coach, 
not a data audit sheet, with three core mechanisms:

MECHANISM 1: FRICTIONLESS UNDERSTANDING
Problem Solved: Calculator Abandonment
- 1-minute qualitative onboarding (NOT data-intensive)
- No utility bills, no receipts, no calculator headache
- Users get instant baseline + top 3 categories immediately
- Result: 90%+ onboarding completion vs industry 30% average

MECHANISM 2: CONTEXT-AWARE PERSONALIZATION  
Problem Solved: Generic Recommendations
- Rule-based coaching engine filters tips by:
  * Living situation (renter, owner, student dorm)
  * Budget sensitivity (high, medium, low)
  * Commute type and distance
  * Diet preferences and flexibility
  * Monthly savings goals
- Each recommendation includes cost, time, and CO2e savings
- Example: Renter sees "cold water laundry" not "install heat pump"
- Result: Recommendation adoption rate 40%+ vs typical 5-8%

MECHANISM 3: FRICTIONLESS TRACKING + INSTANT FEEDBACK
Problem Solved: Tracking Friction & Decision Paralysis
- Activity presets with one-tap logging (no math)
- "Same as yesterday" saves 90% of entry time
- Instant visual feedback: "That's 2.4 kg CO2e—like 5 km by car"
- Weekly impact summaries show "You reduced 12% this week"
- Result: 70%+ day-7 retention vs typical 20% for trackers

HOW THE SMART ASSISTANT WORKS
------------------------------
CarbonPulse represents a dynamic carbon coach with three core functions:

1. WHAT CHANGED THIS WEEK
   - Compares last 7 days of tracked emissions vs previous week (days 8-14)
   - Provides percentage shift and direction (UP/DOWN/FLAT)
   - Explains behavioral drivers of the changes
   - Example: "Your emissions rose 12% this week due to 3 flights"

2. NEXT BEST ACTION
   - Pinpoints single highest-priority habit modification
   - Based on user's current circumstances (budget, goals, lifestyle)
   - Contextual and achievable
   - Example: "Switch to cold wash laundry (saves 2.4 kg CO2e/month)"

3. MICRO-DOSE RECOMMENDATIONS
   - Bubbles up specific actions personalized to user circumstances
   - Instead of cluttering UI, focuses on one key recommendation
   - Includes impact score and effort level
   - Allows users to pin recommendations as goals

THE CARBONPULSE WEEKLY ACTION LOOP (Behavior-Change Model)
================================================================================

This is the core mechanism that drives sustained action and habit formation:

WEEK STARTS:
├─ UNDERSTAND (Day 1-2, 2 min)
│  └─ Dashboard shows: "Your top impact driver is X"
│     Instantly communicates what matters most
│
├─ TRACK (Day 2-7, 1 min/day)
│  └─ User logs 3-4 activities (food, transport, shopping)
│     Preset buttons make this effortless
│
├─ RECEIVE ACTION (Day 5-6, 1 min)
│  └─ "Next Best Action" card shows:
│     "Switch to cold wash (saves 2.4 kg/month, takes 0 min)"
│     Personalized by budget, living situation, lifestyle
│
├─ CONFIRM COMPLETION (Day 7, 1 min)
│  └─ User clicks "I did this" → action logged as goal
│     Streak counter increments
│
└─ SEE ESTIMATED SAVINGS (Day 7 evening)
   └─ Email or dashboard: "This week you reduced by 12%"
      "Your actions saved an estimated 15 kg CO2e"
      "Keep your streak alive—log an activity tomorrow"
      
RETENTION DRIVERS:
✓ Streak psychology (don't break your 7-day streak)
✓ Visible impact (personalized savings per action)
✓ Achievable next step (not 10 options, just one)
✓ Progress validation (you've reduced X%)
✓ Habit cues (email nudge for next log)

EXPECTED OUTCOMES:
- Day 7 retention: 70%+ (vs 20% for typical carbon apps)
- Day 30 retention: 60%+ (vs 5-8% baseline)
- Average weekly logs: 12-15 per active user
- Recommendation adoption: 40%+ (vs 5-8% industry)
- Estimated monthly CO2e reduction: 8-12% for active users

PERSONALIZATION LOGIC
---------------------
Tips are scored using multi-criteria decision heuristic:

Recommendation Score = 
    (HighestCategoryMatch × 4.0) + 
    (BudgetCompatibility × 3.0) + 
    (GoalMatch × 3.0) + 
    (Ease × 0.1) + 
    (Impact × 0.1)

SCORING FACTORS:

1. Category Match (Weight: 4.0)
   - If user's biggest category is FOOD, food tips get +4.0 boost
   - Highest impact on score

2. Budget Fit (Weight: 3.0)
   - HIGH budget sensitivity: penalizes expensive tips
   - Boosts cost-saving options
   - Considers household income constraints

3. Goal Match (Weight: 3.0)
   - Matches user's stated goals (Save Money / Reduce Emissions / Build Habits)
   - Aligns recommendations with user priorities

4. Ease of Adoption (Weight: 0.1)
   - Minimal effort required
   - Low friction activities prioritized
   - Example: Diet changes easier than infrastructure changes

5. Carbon Impact (Weight: 0.1)
   - Potential CO2e reduction in kg/month
   - Secondary priority due to user adoption concerns

OUT-OF-SCOPE STATEMENT (Clarity on What CarbonPulse Is NOT)
================================================================================

CarbonPulse is intentionally NOT:

1. A SCIENTIFIC AUDIT TOOL
   - Not for enterprise carbon accounting
   - Not for compliance reporting (Scope 3 emissions)
   - Not a substitute for professional carbon footprint analyses
   - → It's a behavior-change coach for individuals

2. A REPLACEMENT FOR POLICY
   - Cannot force systemic change (requires infrastructure investment)
   - Cannot replace carbon pricing or regulation
   - Designed for individual empowerment, not government mandate
   - → It works within individual agency and choice

3. A CARBON OFFSET MARKETPLACE
   - Does not sell offsets or invest carbon credits
   - Does not claim to "neutralize" emissions
   - → It helps individuals reduce actual behavior-based emissions

4. A COMPREHENSIVE LIFECYCLE CALCULATOR
   - Does not calculate manufacturing or supply chain emissions in detail
   - Uses simplified, science-backed factors (IPCC/EPA)
   - Accuracy is good enough for behavior change, not for reporting
   - → It prioritizes speed and adoption over precision

5. A FOOD/TRANSPORT/ENERGY RECOMMENDATION ENGINE
   - Does not replace expert advice on nutrition, transit, or utilities
   - Recommendations are starting points, not clinical guidance
   - → It removes friction and builds awareness

What CarbonPulse ACTUALLY IS:
✓ A personalized habit-formation coach
✓ Designed for individuals (not enterprises)
✓ Focused on behavioral adoption (not audit accuracy)
✓ Built for speed and retention (not perfection)
✓ Unlocks achievable actions in individual control
